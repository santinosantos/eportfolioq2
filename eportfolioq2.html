INTRODUCTION

    Hello! My name is Santino Miguel A. Santos , a student from Las Piñas City National Science High School. I was born in Las Piñas City on January 12, 2010 to two loving parents. I first enrolled in Camella Homes Montessori Child Development Center from Nursery to Grade 3. After that, I then transferred to Elizabeth Seton School - Las Piñas Campus where I served the remainder of my Elementary years.

    On Monday, May 16, 2022, I sat the Entrance Test for LPSci. A few weeks later, on June 6, I had successfully passed and started my journey within this institution. I am also known by my nickname Tino and serve currently as the YES-O Public Information Officer, AP Club Grade 9 Level Representative, BERT Transportation Officer, Debate Society Co-Chair, and Class President of 9 - Fairness. I am a fan of Tagalog literature and Southeast Asian culture. I like to play MLBB and watch Netflix in my free time. I know rudimentary Thai and Mandarin Chinese.
